Eurest Token Program
Eurest food tokens are available through Food Services channels. They come in increments of $2, $3, $5 and "Ice Cream product", and are treated as gift cards for purchase price at Boeing cafes.

Available through Boeing's Food Service Caterer, Eurest/Thompson Dining Services, managers often give tokens to employees as a form of recognition.

Tokens can be purchased by completing the form available below and returning it via email to the address indicated on the form. If you are unsure of the appropriate chargeline to use, please contact your Cost Policy focal.
